About Joe Dirt

Early Childhood

The early childhood of Mr. Dirt has yet to be disclosed. We do know that as an infant his skull was left unformed, and his mother made the life altering decision to cover his exposed brain with a mullet wig. During development the wig fused with his skull and he has been unable to receive a hair cut since.According to Joe, his parents abondoned him at the Grand Canyon at age 8 while he was looking for a can of Grizzly chew. His memory of the past is clouded by amnesia, due to multiple head injuries. He does remember having a sister, but does not remember his birth name.

Adulthood

Although having a rough childhood, Joe overcame lifes obstacles with his mantra

"Life's a garden, dig it."

Mr. Dirt went on to acquire success with media sensation detailing his life search for his parents. He found love and married his best friend Brandy. Now he lives in Silvertown and drives his beloved '67 Plymouth GTX Hemi convertible
