Elvis Alaska Park Zeiger

Elvis Alaska Park Zeiger, mononymously known as Elvis, is a Russian-Mexican-American Instagram influencer, notorious con-man, and former doggie daycare manager. He is a Siberian Husky and Samoyed mix. In 2020, he gained notoriety for his Instagram account, elvis_wyd. He is generally white in color but is known to take on the color of his surroundings, for instance mud or grass.

Born: Exact date unknown but biographers point to January 10, 2013 as his date of birth
Birthplace: Corpus Christi, Texas
Weight: 37.5 pounds

Elvis has called a variety of places homes. Born in southern Texas, he moved to Pensacola, Florida for 18 months. From there, he headed to the Northwest for better (colder) weather. He has bounced around the state of Washington, residing in Puyallup, Yelm, Orting, Seattle, Cathlamet, Longview, Kalama, and now, Washougal.
